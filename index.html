📝 Key Aviation Terms

  • PNR: Passenger Name Record (Booking Code)
  • TWOV: Transit Without Visa
  • TIMATIC: Travel Information Manual Automatic
  • PAX: Passengers
  • ETA / ETD: Estimated Time of Arrival / Departure
  • ATA / ATD: Actual Time Of Arrival / Departure
  • STA / STD: Scheduled Time Of Arrival / Departure
  • LDM: Load Distribution Message
  • MVT: Movement Message
  • PSM: Passenger Service Message
  • PTM: Passenger Transfer Message
  • EZFW: Estimated zero fuel weight
  • STPC: Stopover Paid by Carrier (Transit Hotel)
  • DEPO: Passenger being sent back by immigration Deportee
  • WCHR (Wheelchair Ramp): The passenger can walk inside the aircraft but needs a wheelchair for long distances in the airport
  • WCHS (Wheelchair Steps): The passenger cannot walk up or down stairs but can walk short distances inside the aircraft cabin
  • WCHC (Wheelchair Cabin): The passenger is completely immobile and requires a wheelchair and full assistance from check-in to the aircraft seat
